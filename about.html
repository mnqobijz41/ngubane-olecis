Our Story

Building Dreams Since 2017

Ngubane Olecis PTY LTD was founded by Scelo Ngubane with a single vision; to deliver world-class construction and architectural services to the people of KwaZulu-Natal and beyond.

From humble beginnings in Inanda, we have grown into a fully registered, tax-compliant and NHBRC-certified construction company trusted by homeowners, families and businesses across the region.

Our tagline says it all — "You Dream, My Job, Our Vision." We don't just build structures, we bring your ideas to life with precision, quality and care.
